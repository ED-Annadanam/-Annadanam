About ANNADANAM

Food is a basic need of an individual. Annadanam is a social venture which is involved in solving the food security problem. Availability of food resource is not a problem but its management is the main issue. And ANNADANAM is doing the same thing by connecting the food donors and suppliers with the needed ones.
